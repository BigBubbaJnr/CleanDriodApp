Final thoughts

I want to end with something I don't say very often.

When you first told me about this project, I expected it to become "yet another Android cleaner."

I don't think that's what you're building anymore.

I think you're building something closer to "Files by Google for power users"—a storage analysis tool with a distinctive identity rather than a utility that promises impossible system optimizations.

If you keep following that philosophy, I think you'll end up with a more trustworthy and sustainable product.

And one last piece of advice: I would seriously consider changing the name from "CleanDriod" to "CleanDroid." I noticed the current spelling is "Driod" throughout the project. If that's unintentional, I'd fix it before launch. A consistent, correctly spelled name will make your branding, Play Store listing, and word-of-mouth recommendations much stronger. It's much easier to make that change now than after you've published the app.
